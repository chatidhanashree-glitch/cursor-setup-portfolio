# Podcast: Rand Fishkin on SEO, AI Overviews, and the Future of Content
Guest: Rand Fishkin
Link: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=5JQvdLYvGZI

## Summary
Rand Fishkin discusses how AI is changing search behavior, especially with AI Overviews and zero-click searches. He explains that traditional SEO strategies are becoming less effective as users increasingly get answers directly from AI without clicking websites.

## Key Insights

- AI search is reducing website clicks through zero-click results
- Visibility in AI answers is becoming more important than traditional rankings
- Engagement signals may be more important than backlinks in modern SEO :contentReference[oaicite:1]{index=1}
- SEO strategies must adapt to changes in how users consume information
- Brand visibility across multiple platforms is becoming critical

---

## My Observations

- SEO is shifting from traffic generation → brand visibility
- AI tools are changing user behavior, not just search results
- Traditional SEO metrics (rankings, clicks) are becoming less reliable
- This reinforces the need for multi-channel presence (LinkedIn, content, etc.)
- There is a strong shift toward **authority + visibility + trust**

## Why This Matters for AI SEO

This podcast highlights that AI SEO is not just about optimizing content, but about:
- Being present in AI-generated answers
- Building authority across platforms
- Adapting to zero-click search behavior
