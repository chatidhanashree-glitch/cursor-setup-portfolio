## Post 1
Topic: AI Overviews and content credibility

### Summary
Lily highlights that AI-generated search results (like Google AI Overviews) sometimes cite low-quality or misleading sources, raising concerns about content trust.

### Key Insights
- AI search can surface inaccurate or low-quality content
- Credibility and trust signals are becoming more important
- Platforms like LinkedIn are increasingly cited in AI results
- E-E-A-T (Experience, Expertise, Authority, Trust) is critical

## Post 2
Topic: Impact of Google algorithm updates

### Summary
Lily explains that even high-performing websites can lose traffic after algorithm updates, showing how unpredictable SEO can be.

### Key Insights
- Google updates can significantly affect rankings
- SEO is influenced by external and uncontrollable factors
- Strong sites are not immune to traffic drops
- Diversification beyond SEO is important

## My Overall Takeaways

- Trust and authority are essential in modern SEO
- AI search still has reliability challenges
- SEO strategies must adapt to constant algorithm changes
- Content quality and credibility are more important than ever
