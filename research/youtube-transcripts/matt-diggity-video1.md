# Video: I did AI-SEO for ChatGPT and Google AI. Here’s what happened…
Link: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=4GBlHObjOrY

## Key Insights

- AI SEO focuses on getting featured in AI overviews and ChatGPT results, not just Google rankings
- Content should be direct, concise, and answer-first (avoid fluff)
- Conversational writing performs better for AI-based search queries
- Proper heading structure (H1, H2, H3) helps AI understand content
- Adding TLDR or key takeaway sections improves chances of being cited
- Brand trust and reputation play a major role in AI visibility
- Backlinks and authority signals are still important for AI SEO
- Tracking AI traffic using analytics is essential for optimization

---

## My Observations

- AI search is shifting from keyword-based to intent-based queries
- UX (clarity, structure, readability) is becoming more important than ever
- SEO is no longer just ranking — it’s about being selected by AI systems
- Strong connection between content design (UX) and SEO performance
