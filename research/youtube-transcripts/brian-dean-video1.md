# Video: The 8-Step SEO Strategy for Higher Rankings
Link: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=OUqMSy_vNys

## Key Insights

- SEO starts with **keyword research**, especially long-tail keywords with lower competition
- High-quality, in-depth content performs better than short or generic content
- On-page SEO (title tags, headings, structure) is critical for rankings
- User experience (readability, layout, engagement) directly impacts SEO performance
- Backlinks remain one of the strongest ranking factors
- Content promotion is as important as content creation
- Consistency and updating content improves long-term rankings
- SEO success requires combining **content + optimization + authority building**

---

## My Observations

- Even with AI SEO, fundamentals like backlinks and content quality still matter
- UX design (structure, readability) plays a huge role in SEO success
- AI tools can speed up content creation, but strategy remains human-driven
- There is a clear overlap between **UI/UX thinking and SEO performance**
- SEO is not one tactic — it's a system of multiple steps working together
