## Key Insights

- AI search (like Perplexity) is shifting from search engines to **answer engines**
- Traditional SEO rankings matter less; **citations in AI answers** are the new goal
- “Parasite SEO” = publishing on high-authority platforms (Medium, LinkedIn, Quora)
- AI prefers **clear, structured, answer-first content**
- Keyword stuffing is ineffective — **intent-based, conversational queries matter**
- Fresh content is critical; outdated content loses visibility quickly
- AI prioritizes **content with sources and credibility signals**
- Testing content in AI tools before publishing improves success rate

## My Observations

- SEO is evolving from “ranking on Google” → “being referenced by AI”
- UX writing (clarity, structure, readability) is becoming a core SEO skill
- Platforms like LinkedIn and Medium are becoming more powerful due to AI indexing
- There is a strong shift toward **real-time, dynamic content updates**
- Authority is no longer just backlinks — it's **trust + clarity + freshness**
